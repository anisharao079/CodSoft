ARAKU VALLEY:

Araku Valley, popularly known as Araku, is a scenic hill station in the Vishakhapatnam district of Andhra Pradesh.
Araku Valley is surrounded by tall mountains and foggy clouds.
The numerous waterfalls and coffee plantations make it an ideal getaway.
It's surrounded by the thick forests of the Eastern Ghats mountain range.
